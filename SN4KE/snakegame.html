Rules Of Snaker Snake

  • The game is infinite which means it has no end and you can play as long as you want.

  • To gain points you need to feed on the tiny circles that appear on the screen. Note that when a circle spawns on the canvas, it disapperas after 20 seconds if you don't eat it.

  • Unlike the typical Snake Xenzia, you don't lose when you go out of the screen. You will loose however if any part of your body makes contact with itself.

  • Have fun!!!
